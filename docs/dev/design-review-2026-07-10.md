# Design-quality review — 2026-07-10

**Method.** Two-phase funnel, adversarially verified. Phase 1: an 8-scout *wrong-model smell census*
(bug-churn shapes, parallel writers, containment accretion, hand-rolled-vs-standard, coupling, dead
schema, FE patterns, fired-trigger deferrals) ranked 15 candidates. Phase 2: 9 deep model reviews on

industry parity). 17 agents, ~1.6M tokens, 438 tool reads. This doc is the curated program of record;
per-area detail (current-model reconstructions with file:line, steelmanned alternatives, verifier
reasoning) lives in the session transcript.

**Headline.** Design health is strong — 6 of 11 areas KEEP with verified, citable defenses; 3 confirmed net-deleting redesigns (tax-fact value type, one post-finalize collect policy, required engine deps) go to the owner; the two urgent money items are amending pushed commit 2b3069f before merge and the proration deferred-tax hotfix.

## Census summary

The repo's overall design health is strong — the census found no rot in the crown-jewel money semantics themselves (credits ledger, rating math, payment-settlement truth, RLS all came back clean or deliberately-layered) — but it exposed a consistent meta-pattern: where a model is wrong-shaped, this codebase contains rather than dissolves, accreting lints, mirror comments, backstop sweeps, and per-instance fixes. Three mega-clusters dominate: invoice-issuance duplication (6 of 8 scouts converged: ~6-14 hand-synced writers for headers/tax facts/collect/lines, with two live defects verified by the ranker — the threshold no-PM arm silently does nothing, and the proration writer drops TaxDeferredAt/TaxPendingReason/TaxErrorCode), simulated-time cohabitation (7 independent containment layers, ~23 fix commits, 4 ADR generations), and narrated-effects-instead-of-recorded-facts (attention banners lying twice in one week + 12 remaining post-commit Dispatch sites). The census also caught a live process failure: the ADR-084/085 recipe dissolution — cited to scouts as a completed precedent — is verified stranded on two unmerged branches while main still ships the superseded conformance-gate machinery, and the claimed ADR numbers are invisible to the documented `ls docs/adr/` numbering check. Secondary findings: an undocumented build-vs-buy blind spot in infra (hand-rolled SMTP/MIME with 3 shipped bug classes, an 1107-line migration-runner hybrid mirroring library internals, Redis with triple fallback machinery), and a frontend that lags backend discipline by roughly a year (dual API/type systems, fetch-all-filter-client truncation bugs on money surfaces, zero tests in CI). Many acknowledged deferrals checked out sound (ADR-062 core, payment-settlement stack, coupon cut), which is itself a health signal — but three scouts independently produced evidence that ADR-062's own count-based consolidation trigger may have quietly fired.

**Crown jewels:** IMPLICATED by the census: (1) billing cycle — hardest hit; candidates 1, 4, 9, and 14 all live in cycle/threshold/proration issuance, with two ranker-verified live defects (threshold no-PM collect gap at threshold_scan.go:879-891; proration writer missing 3 deferred-tax fields) plus the engine's nil-collaborator accounting fallbacks (candidate 7); (2) test clocks — candidate 2 is a top-3 finding: 7 containment layers and a post-ADR-086 fix burst defending cohabitation of simulated rows in production tables. PARTIALLY implicated: payment truth — the settlement/reconciler stack itself was explicitly vetted by the containment scout as mapping 1:1 to real Stripe failure modes (prior audits hold), but two divergent 'PM ready' predicates (candidate 4) and the webhook-mirror ordering question are residual edges. CLEAN: credits ledger (event-sourced model unflagged by all 8 scouts; only adjacent CN tax-reversal recovery timing appears, inside candidate 13); rating (decimal-rate math ADR-045 unflagged; only its application is re-implemented across the 4 line builders, folded into candidate 4); RLS (zero findings on tenant isolation — the only tenant-adjacent item is the unenforced tenants.status lifecycle in candidate 15, which is not an isolation issue).

## KEEP verdicts — defenses to record (each becomes an ADR amendment / doc)

- [Test-clock sim isolation → ADR-086 amendment] Reject the per-clock RLS/connection-layer scope dimension: the livemode analogy breaks at its load-bearing point (livemode is caller-derived and boundary-bindable; sim-ness is data-derived — operators must see sim+real together, badged), so the three-valued scope default is unwinnable in either direction; Stripe (docs verified 2026-07-10) runs exactly Velox's model — sandbox cohabitation, customer pin, filter-based sweep carve-out, delete-tears-down-customers.
- [Attention-banner copy → ADR-009 amendment] Banner Messages may assert only input-provable facts and engine dispositions, never past-tense per-invoice effects; recorded effects render exclusively from ledger-backed surfaces (invoice timeline, Sent emails) which cannot lie; ledger-derived banner facts (alt A) recorded decided-but-deferred (~50-line adoption path pre-exists) with trigger = a DP reports banner-vs-timeline triage confusion; copy stays in internal/domain because Message is public wire contract (Stripe-parity server-authored envelope).
- [Dunning creation → ADR-064 amendment, rejected alternative 4] In-tx run creation in the fail-transition tx loses on derivability (run existence/schedule is a pure function of committed invoice state — the sweep is the projection-maintainer, not a patch), forces a forbidden cross-domain tx surface, inverts reliability (dunning failure would 500 the Stripe webhook), kills config-change self-healing, and deletes zero resolve sites; the 463429a one-day revert condemns edge-hooked persisted projections without a level leg — it validates this model. Resolution deliberately shares the same triggered-primary + derived-backstop grammar.
- [Proration peers → ADR-048 second amendment] A mid-period upgrade against a PAID in_advance base must produce a peer invoice: finalized-invoice immutability + invoice-anchored tax reversal (per-invoice caps ⇒ allocation) outrank single-funder; immediate-charge is the B2B norm (Stripe always_invoice, Orb new-invoice arm, Lago code-verified second in-period invoice; 7/7 platforms keep the anchor); the funding-set machinery is small, gated by lint-funding-set.sh, and the bug class has not recurred since 2026-06-15.
- [Threshold two-mode → ADR-065/066 amendment] A threshold fire is not an early cycle close: keep-anchor is the industry's primary semantics (Stripe's documented default; Lago/Orb/Metronome's only mode — Lago ships the same watermark); the carve-outs are mode-inherent (dual-subtotal survives reset=true or a steady max-meter refires per tick; early close converts split-billing into customer-adverse per-window peak billing); ADR-031's prepaid in_advance base pins the reset=false boundary. Revisit trigger: a charge-without-invoice/applied-balance primitive.
- [Line builders → recorded in the collect-policy ADR] Separate cycle/cancel/on-create/preview/threshold builders are deliberate: five ADR-anchored divergent window semantics; unification = 6-flag monolith re-risking ~15 individually-annotated fixes (SOLID audit already ruled the shape deliberate); the builders' real bug class dies via the two surgical fix-nows instead.
- [Cold-read grill → docs/dev/hard-questions.md] Consolidate the layered-defense story (durable-state idempotency key + unknown-classifier + reconciler; response-cache middleware + domain unique constraints; BeginTx livemode refusal + strict-mode panic + fan-out asserts; boot-time PgBouncer topology probe) plus the two written scope cuts with triggers (late-event true-up, RBAC/2FA) — ~1 hour, most answers already exist as code comments; high hiring-reader value.

## Principle amendments (for the playbook / working rules)

- [Playbook class J — rendered/asserted truth] Every surface that ASSERTS a money/effect fact (banner copy, log line, email, dashboard aggregate, README) derives it from the authoritative record or states engine behavior — never re-derives heuristically. Fix-kinds: plumb-the-value / stamp-at-write (m0142 pattern) / required-anchor type (ADR-086 §4 pattern). Detection: 'every RENDERER of the state' becomes item 6 of the §2 site-set. Test-lock: pure-logic display-helper tests (effectiveNow.test.ts seed). Closes the class (7+ recurrences) that structurally escaped the write-side-only site-set.
- [Containment trip-wire — sequences mechanized-invariant vs complexity-accretion] On a recurring bug shape, first ask what model change deletes the class; mechanize a gate only when the minimal complete model still permits the mistake AND the language can't express the invariant (Go lint = legitimate; TS branded type = preferred dissolve). TRIP-WIRE: any PR shipping a SECOND gate/backstop/sweep-twin/honesty-flag on one shape must contain a written one-paragraph model trial in its ADR, pass or fail. (Three in-repo cascades — TZ ×4 ADRs, FE-time ×4 PRs, recipe gate + 2 stranded ADRs — would each have collapsed earlier.)
- [no-silent-fallbacks extension — typed effect outcomes] Effect interfaces return done|failed|skipped(reason); returning nil for a skipped effect is forbidden; a skipped effect leaves a queryable trace; no surface may assert an effect whose outcome it cannot read. PROSPECTIVE + exactly one retrofit (NotifyNoPaymentMethod) — explicitly NOT a repo-wide effect-interface migration.
- [no-belt-and-suspenders clarification] One LOAD-BEARING mechanism per requirement: a latency-primary over a correctness-backstop is not redundancy — two mechanisms both claiming correctness for the same failure mode is. Records ADR-064 (and the resolve grammar) as conforming, ending the doctrine 'contradiction'.

## Verdict

Overall design health is genuinely strong: six of eleven areas survived adversarial review outright, several with showcase-grade answers already written into the code (PgBouncer boot probe, charge idempotency layering), and every KEEP now has a verified defense worth committing as an ADR amendment so none of it gets re-litigated. The bad news is concentrated and money-shaped: one live defect (deferred-tax proration invoices permanently invisible to the auto-retry reconciler) and one about-to-ship regression (2b3069f's Finalized gate strands tax-pending threshold invoices) — do those two first, today; 2b3069f is verified still unmerged so the amendment is free. The three confirmed redesigns share one signature — hand-copied fact/effect propagation breeding drift — and all three net-delete code with no schema or behavior change, so they are unusually cheap for their payoff; recommended order is TaxFacts (~1 day, absorbs the hotfix), then CollectAfterFinalize (absorbs 2b3069f and fixes three defects as by-products), then EngineDeps (3 staged PRs, should land before any HA second-composition-root work). The strongest single pattern across all eleven reviews: censuses kept proposing structural machinery (RLS scope dimensions, unified builders, tx-required-only signatures) where the verified failures were actually one-string, one-field, or one-block copy drift — the razor held, and the refuted appendix is the proof to cite next time. The principles corpus needs its three amendments (rendered-truth class J, containment trip-wire, typed effect outcomes) because the repo demonstrably dissolves wrong models but 2-4 containment layers too late; these are doc-level plus one small interface retrofit, not a build program. Nothing in the triggered backlog should be built now — every item has a precise, checkable trigger, and writing those triggers down is what converts scope cuts from skeletons into decisions an expert evaluator respects.
